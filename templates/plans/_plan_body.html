{% comment %} Shared shareable-plan body partial (issue #440, extended in #499). Used by both ``plans/member_plan_detail.html`` (read-only, viewed by teammates) and ``plans/my_plan_detail.html`` (owner edit surface). The two templates differ only in the visibility-toggle form at the top and in the "View cohort board" CTA -- everything else is rendered here. DO NOT render ``InterviewNote`` rows in this partial. Internal and external interview notes are deliberately omitted from member-facing views in this issue. Issue #499 adds participant ``WeekNote`` rendering inside each week. Notes show owner-authored body text + a relative timestamp. The owner (``plan_can_edit`` is True) sees an add-note textarea, plus inline edit/delete controls on their own notes. Teammates on cohort plans see the notes read-only with no edit/delete affordances. Empty weeks render the "no notes yet" copy on the owner page; on the read-only teammate view the empty state is silent (no notes section emitted) to keep the page tight when a week has nothing to say. Required context: - ``plan`` (with related ``weeks``, ``resources``, ``deliverables``, ``next_steps`` prefetched by the view; ``weeks__notes__author`` prefetched on the views that need notes). - ``plan_can_edit`` (boolean): whether the viewer is the owner. {% endcomment %} {% load accounts_extras plan_markdown %}
